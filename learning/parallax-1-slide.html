Austrian Post is a traditional company, but also a forward-looking technology leader. They’ve contacted Omnio as they started providing banking services and wanted to expand digitally.

Users can create wallets where they can save up money for vacays or that vila on a beach in Italy. Wait, that’s me.

The app had to be delivered in German language. But since none of us spoke German, we wrote all the copies in English so it’s easily understood what elements we’re referring to when syncing between designers –– developers –– testers –– PMs.

Had an excel document with translations from English to German from the Post AG people. It was fun seeing how long the german words are and nice that we could test the designs with longer words.

Overall, the app provides a clear and basic user experience. It can be easily plugged into other banks without being recognized and that’s exactly what the client was looking for.
