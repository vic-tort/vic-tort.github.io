VRV.CO is a video streaming platform that collects fandom based entertainment into one convenient spot.

2018

Before working with VRV, i had no idea the fandom audience is so big. I used to think that anime is just animated pictures we’ve all used to watch in the childhood. While in fact, animated pictures can be way more expressive than movies, hence why there are so many anime related to extra-terrestrial creatures and magical powers.

Working on VRV was my first experience working within a product company being surrounded by a big team of designers and engineers lead by one mission. To launch.

The team was distributed both in San Francisco and Chisinau. VRV.co is available on nearly all existing platforms that can run apps.

My role. I've been working with two other designers on the web platform.

I was responsible for improving existing features and coming up with new ones. Worked on several payments projects related to subscription funnels. Took ownership with the creation of the web platform design system.

More info will be added to this case soon.
